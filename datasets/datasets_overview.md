# Dataset Overview | Gambaran Dataset

## CRM Datasets

### `cust_info.csv`
**EN:** Customer master data  
**ID:** Data master pelanggan  

**Main Columns:**
- `cst_id`
- `cst_key`
- `cst_firstname`
- `cst_lastname`
- `cst_marital_status`
- `cst_gndr`
- `cst_create_date`

---

### `prd_info.csv`
**EN:** Product master data  
**ID:** Data master produk  

**Main Columns:**
- `prd_id`
- `prd_key`
- `prd_nm`
- `prd_cost`
- `prd_line`
- `prd_start_dt`
- `prd_end_dt`

---

### `sales_details.csv`
**EN:** Sales transaction data  
**ID:** Data transaksi penjualan  

**Main Columns:**
- `sls_ord_num`
- `sls_prd_key`
- `sls_cust_id`
- `sls_order_dt`
- `sls_ship_dt`
- `sls_due_dt`
- `sls_sales`
- `sls_quantity`
- `sls_price`

---
